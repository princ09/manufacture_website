    About Us

    Technofine Engineers is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company and a premier manufacturer and supplier of Flanges, Pipe Fittings & Tube Fittings to the petrochemical, chemical process, offshore & shipbuilding industries.

    Today Technofine Engineers has evolved into one of the best in the Industry with single source responsibility to our satisfied customers. Behind our Reliable, affordable, & high quality solutions is a team of skilled, trained and experienced people dedicated to ensure total customer satisfaction.

    We pride ourselves on our long term relationships with the world's finest producers. All products conform with ASTM, ANSI, AWWA, DIN, JIS, UNI, BS, IS and other applicable standards and most of the firms that we work with are ISO 9000 certified.
